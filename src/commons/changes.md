### The Necessity of Time Distribution
```
→ necessity compels: accurate apportionment of time
→ time apportionment = allocation across different kinds of work
→ Society must distribute its time in a purposeful way to achieve a production adequate to its overall needs
→ Society/Individual must distribute its time correctly in order to achieve knowledge in proper proportions or in order to satisfy the various demands on its activity
```

### Time Allocation Constraints
```
→ Σ(time allocated to branch i) = total social time
→ Σ(time for work type i) = total available time for work type i
→ allocation must satisfy: production ≥ needs
```

### Proportional Allocation Formulas
```
→ proportion of time to branch i = time allocated to branch i / total social time
→ share of branch i in total time = time_i / Σ(all time)
→ Σ(labor time in branch i) distributed among the various branches of production to produce enough to satisfy Σ(needs)
→ share of labor to branch i = time_i / Σ(time_j for all branches j)
→ time_i = f(difficulty_i, importance_i, total_available_time)
```

## 3. Individual Labor and Shares

### Individual Labor as Part of the Whole
```
→ individual labor exists directly as component part of total labor
→ individual labor power = portion of total social labor power
→ individual's share of social labor = individual labor / total social labor
→ portion = individual labor time / Σ(social labor time)
→ duration = measure of expenditure of labor power (naturally social)
```

### What Individual Labor Buys
```
→ labor buys: share of communal production (NOT specific product)
→ individual's share of communal production = f(individual's labor contribution)
→ share = individual's labor / total social labor
→ all products = share of communal production
```

### Participation in Consumption
```
→ organization of labor = communal consumption
→ individual's consumption share = f(organization of labor)
```

### Comparing Labor Across Branches
```
→ comparison across branches: use average labor time
→ average labor time for type τ = Σ(labor time in τ) / count(workers in τ)
```
